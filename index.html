Hello! I’m Mohamed a passionate programmer, UI/UX designer, and graphic designer with a keen eye for detail and a love for creating seamless digital experiences. My journey in the tech world began with a fascination for coding, and I’ve since honed my skills in HTML and CSS to build responsive and visually appealing websites. Beyond my technical skills, I’m an avid learner who enjoys keeping up with the latest design trends and technologies. When I’m not immersed in design projects, you can find me exploring nature, experimenting with photography, or diving into the world of indie games. I believe in the power of creativity and technology to shape our experiences, and I’m excited to continue this journey of innovation and inspiration.
